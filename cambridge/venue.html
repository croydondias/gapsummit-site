Venue

The GapSummit will take place at the University of Cambridge.

Founded in 1209, the University of Cambridge is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the world. Its mission is to contribute to society through the pursuit of education, learning, and research at the highest international levels of excellence. We look forward to welcoming our speakers and delegates to this setting where together we will be able to challenge, grow and connect.

More details on venue and accommodation will be available in due course.
