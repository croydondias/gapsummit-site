Programme

During the three days of the GapSummit the leaders of tomorrow will learn from and challenge current leaders in the bio-economy through a program which is dedicated to addressing 7 gaps in biotechnology:

  1. Research and Innovation Gap
  2. Funding Gap
  3. Future Health Gap
  4. Future Resources Gap
  5. People Gap
  6. Bioethics Gap
  7. Public Perception and Education Gap

The full GapSummit 2016 programme will be released in due course. Meanwhile feel free to browse the GapSummit 2014 programme and read on below for more details on the overall structure of the GapSummit.

Programme Overview

Speaking Sessions

Keynotes

The GapSummit programme will include keynote talks where the most prestigious industry and research representatives will present their views on a particular aspect of the bioeconomy. The audience will also have the opportunity to pose challenging questions directly to speakers.

Panel Sessions

Each day there will be panel sessions covering the ‘7 Gaps’ in biotechnology. Speakers on the panel have been carefully selected to represent a cross-section of views on each of these important topics. Audience members will be encouraged to put their ideas for solving some of the challenges in each field, directly to the speakers.

Workshops

In addition to keynote talks and panel sessions, there will also be workshops to provide participants with the opportunity to have a highly in-depth discussion with experts in particular topics of interest ranging from Intellectual Property to Commercialising Research.

Voices of Tomorrow - business plan competition

The Voices of Tomorrow business plan competition is a GapSummit initiative created to support breakthrough solutions to future global bio-economy challenges from leaders of tomorrow in the biotechnology and life science fields worldwide.

If you are an aspiring global leader in the biotech and life science industry and have an innovative idea or solution that can transform the future global bio-economy, the GapSummit and Voices of Tomorrow business plan competition is right for you. We are looking for students, young professionals and leaders in the field of biotechnology/life sciences who are passionate, dedicated and ready to launch solutions to one or more of the 7 Gaps in biotechnology.

From January 2016, GapSummit attendees will have the opportunity to work together in teams under an experienced mentor to develop and submit an initial project proposal about their impactful solution(s).

The teams with the most promising innovations will be invited to pitch their business or solutions to a prestigious panel of judges at the GapSummit, with the best team(s) winning prizes including seed funding and mentoring opportunities.

Networking

In addition to the networking opportunities in coffee and lunch breaks, the GapSummit will be hosting dinners in some of the most prestigious venues in Cambridge. These dinners shall provide the opportunity to enjoy a delicious meal, whilst connecting with speakers, sponsors and your fellow leaders of tomorrow.

Accommodation will be provided from the Sunday 3rd April until lunch time on Thursday 7th April. GapSummit shall therefore be organising social activities on both the Sunday and Thursday, to allow you time to forge strong relationships with your peers as well as to experience iconic Cambridge activities such as punting on the River Cam.
