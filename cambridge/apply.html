Who should apply?

GapSummit will be welcoming applications from Leaders of Tomorrow across all regions of the globe. We are seeking enthusiastic students, post-docs, entrepreneurs and early-stage professionals.

Those passionate about discussing the current global bioeconomy and the grand challenges biotech will need to solve by 2050 with leading industry experts, policy makers and researcher pioneers are warmly invited to submit an application.

Please note: applicants will need to be available to be at the University of Cambridge, UK from the 3rd to 7th April 2016. Whilst the GapSummit covers accommodation and most meals throughout the GapSummit itself, travel bursaries will only be considered in exceptional circumstances as applicants are encouraged to seek alternative sources of funding to cover travel expenses.

Application dates

Applications for GapSummit 2016 will open on the 19th of September and close on the 29th of November 2015. Given that places are limited, applicants are strongly advised to apply before the interim October 25th deadline as the strongest candidates will be made offers at this point.

7 reasons why you should apply

  1. Be selected as an ambassador to represent your country on a global platform.
  2. A great networking opportunity not only to meet Leaders of Today (CEOs of top biotech companies, venture capitalists, policy makers, research pioneers) but also to develop an global network with international peers.
  3. Great opportunity to meet potential employers, to find out about career opportunities with the most successful and exciting companies and academic institutions.
  4. Develop your long term career plans with the trends being predicted for the future and opportunities highlighted at the summit.
  5. Build your own awareness of the bigger global biotechnology picture.
  6. Develop solutions to improve the gaps in the global bioeconomy.
  7. Have fun! Meet amazing people, dine and network in the finest college in University of Cambridge.

Selection process

The GapSummit Advisory Board will supporting the selection of Leaders of Tomorrow who show a passion for resolving the grand challenges in biotechnology by:

  1. Engaging with current leaders on the major issues in the bioeconomy.
  2. Undertaking projects to help resolve some of the biggest gaps in the industry.

Once a candidate has submitted an application, it will be reviewed and candidates may be invited for an online interview. To increase your chances of receiving a place, it is strongly encouraged that you submit your application before the October 25th interim deadline, as well as participate in the Voices of Tomorrow business plan competition. If you wish to participate in the GapSummit and the Voices of Tomorrow competition as part of an already established group, groups of up to 4 people are welcome to apply. However, we will require separate applications from each of the group's members and individual offers will be made to attend the GapSummit on the basis of individual merit.

Applications will close on the 29th November and all final offers will be made by December 21st 2015.
